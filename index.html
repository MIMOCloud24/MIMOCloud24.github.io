About Me

Hi, I’m Mimoune Tahri, a tech entrepreneur and cybersecurity student based in Orlando, Florida. I have a diverse background in networking, IT support, and cybersecurity, having worked in various roles from help desk technician to IT security specialist.

I’m currently pursuing my Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity Technology at UMGC and have already completed certifications like CompTIA A+ and Google IT Support. My journey doesn’t stop there—I’m aiming for more certifications like Network+,security+, Cloud+, AWS certifications, and CCNA, with a long-term goal of becoming a Cloud Security Architect.

Born in Morocco and now living in the U.S., I bring a multicultural perspective to my work. My career in IT started from humble beginnings, growing a home-based computer repair business, Techfix Solutions, into a successful venture. I’m passionate about helping others and sharing my knowledge through hands-on projects, which I’ll be documenting on this site.

In my spare time, I enjoy lifelong learning, working on personal projects, and spending time with my family. I believe in the power of technology to create meaningful solutions and make a positive impact.
